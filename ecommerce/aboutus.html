WHO WE ARE


LifeStyle is an Indian ecommerce company with headquarters in Bhagalpur. It is the largest Internet-based retailer in the India. Lifestyle started selling mobilephones,cameras,apparels. LifeStyle also sells certain low-end products like USB cables and other accessories.

OUR HISTORY

2015
The company was founded in 2015, spurred by what we called "Initiating framework", which described our efforts as an initiate to participate in the Internet business boom during that time. In 2016, we began to work on a business plan for what would eventually become LifeStyle.

2016
In January 2016, LifeStyle has received a funding of $12 million from Venture Partners and Indo-US Venture Partners.
